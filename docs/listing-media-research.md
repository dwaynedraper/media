## SUPPORTING VERIFIED STATS (not in the diagram, safe to use with year labels)

- **Redfin DSLR study (2013, 22 markets):** pro-photographed homes $200K–$1M sold for $3,400–$11,200 more vs list; $400Ks sold 21 days faster; sharpest 10% of photos sold at/above list 44% vs 13% for average sharpness. [PLAT] Always date it: "Redfin, 2013."
- **Zillow listing-photo count (2015):** <9 photos → ~20% less likely to sell in 60 days vs 22–27 photos; 28+ slightly slower than 22–27. [PLAT]
- **Zillow first-week views (2015):** <100 first-week views → ~12% chance of selling in 60 days; 280+ views → ~36%. [PLAT]
- **NAR Generational Trends (Apr 2025):** floor plans "very useful" peaks at 61% for buyers 45–59; never below 51% in any age group. [IND]
- **Zillow floor-plan friction (2021–22):** 56% of buyers wasted time touring homes they'd have skipped with a floor plan; ~75% of sellers call a floor plan highly important. [PLAT, press-release framing]
- **Zillow 3D Home (2021/2022):** 32% more saves (May 2020 data); 43% more views / 55% more saves (Nov 2021–Jan 2022 data). For the 3D add-on, not Essentials. [PLAT]
- **Zillow CHTR 2023:** 72% of buyers say 3D tours beat static photos for feel of space; 67% wish more listings had them. [PLAT]
- **Matterport / Texas Tech (2020, 143,575 listings):** 3D-tour listings closed 4–9% higher, up to 31% faster. Vendor-published, preliminary — always say "up to" and "Matterport-commissioned." [VEN]
- **HomeJab agent survey (2021, n=289):** 40% of agents actively use twilight photos; 76% have used or are interested; twilight skews to $500K+ listings (59%). [VEN, real survey]
- **Zillow Showcase (Oct 2025 data):** +79% views, +76% saves, +91% shares, 10% more likely pending in 14 days, ~2% higher sale price — but bundles floor plan + paid exposure; can't be attributed to any one media type. [PLAT/promotional]
- **BoxBrownie listing audit (2021, 25,000 listings, via NAR Magazine):** 94% of listings have no true virtual tour; <30% follow photography best practices; only 16% include a floor plan. [VEN] (CubiCasa, Oct 2025: floor plans now on ~1 in 3 new listings. [VEN])

## Notable gaps (honest limitations)

- No MLS-scale 2022–2026 study isolates professional vs amateur photography. Most recent rigorous large-sample work is Zillow's Mar 2025 engagement analysis (S4).
- No portal or MLS has ever published twilight-specific performance data.
- No independent study isolates aerial imagery's effect on sale outcomes — the differentiation argument (S5+S6: most agents value it, few shoots include it) is the honest version.
- Reels engagement data (S7) is cross-industry, not real-estate-specific.
