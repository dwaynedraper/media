# Platform-Conferred Media Advantages — "If You List on X, This Media Gets Rewarded"

Compiled June 2026 for Sharp Sighted Media. Companion to `listing-media-research.md`.
Every claim below was fetched and confirmed verbatim on the platform's OWN property
(product page, help center, press release) unless flagged. Tags: **[OFFICIAL]** explicit
platform commitment · **[OFFICIAL-STAT]** platform data, correlational · **[INFERENCE]**
plausible but the platform never says it — be honest when pitching · **[PAID]** bought, not earned.

---

## "I use Zillow for all my listings" → the strongest play in the file

**1. Zillow interactive floor plan = "prioritized placement in search results." [OFFICIAL]**
Zillow's own 3D Home FAQ, live (modified May 2026): *"Listings with Zillow interactive
floor plans will receive prioritized placement in search results on Zillow."*
→ https://www.zillow.com/3d-home/faq/

**Critical nuance:** this applies to Zillow's **interactive floor plan**, which can only be
created with the **Zillow 3D Home app + a compatible 360 camera** (Ricoh Theta Z1/X/SC2/V,
Insta360 X2/X3/X4/X5/ONE RS 1-inch). A LiDAR/CubiCasa-style floor plan uploaded as an
image does NOT trigger this. The 3D Home capture is free — no per-listing or hosting fees.

**2. 3D Home tours get filter + tag + email exposure. [OFFICIAL — and quotable]**
Zillow's photographer marketing page (Zillow-approved copy, licensed for reuse):
*"listings with 3D Home tours and interactive floor plans get premium placement on Zillow
and Trulia"* via *"unique search tags, special search filters and dedicated emails to buyers."*
→ https://www.zillow.com/agents/zillow-3d-home-best-practices/
Engagement stats on Zillow's current page (Oct 2022–Mar 2023 data, top 50 MSAs): floor-plan
listings saw **60% more views, 79% more saves, 72% more shares**. [OFFICIAL-STAT]

**3. One capture, two portals. [OFFICIAL]**
Since the Oct 2024 Zillow–Realtor.com agreement, Zillow 3D Home tours + interactive floor
plans **auto-syndicate to Realtor.com**. One 360 capture earns interactive-media display on
both major portals. → zillowgroup.com newsroom, Oct 22, 2024.

**4. Matterport is OUT at Zillow. [OFFICIAL-adjacent]**
October 2025: Zillow removed Matterport tours entirely (CoStar dispute; reported by Inman,
HousingWire, RISMedia with Zillow statements — not on a Zillow page). Even before that,
Zillow's help docs demoted third-party tours to a hyperlink while 3D Home takes the prominent
slot. Net: **Zillow 3D Home is now effectively the only first-class tour format on Zillow.**

**5. Zillow Showcase — the only explicit paid priority-placement product. [PAID]**
Official page: *"Priority placement in search and map results,"* larger map images, dedicated
buyer email alerts, **capped at 10% of listings per market**. Current official comparison:
+79% page views, +76% saves, +91% shares vs similar nearby listings (Apr 2025, methodology
footnoted). Showcase **requires** exactly photographer-deliverable media: 3D Home app tour +
interactive floor plan + high-res stills delivered via Aryeo (Zillow-owned).
**Any photographer can shoot Showcase** — *"Agents have the freedom to select their preferred
photographer"* — after meeting tech requirements (free Aryeo Lite + 360 camera). "Showcase
Select" is a paid Aryeo Pro discoverability badge, not a gate.

**6. What Zillow does NOT say. [keep us honest]**
No official statement that listing engagement (saves/views) boosts rank in "Homes for You"
(that sort is based on the *shopper's* behavior). Premier Agent buys lead routing, not listing
placement. Don't pitch either.

**The pitch, in one breath:** "If Zillow is your storefront: a 360 capture I deliver gets your
listing Zillow's own 'prioritized placement,' the 3D filter/tag, buyer emails, and the same
interactive media on Realtor.com — and it's the exact media package required if you ever want
Showcase, which Zillow caps at 10% of your market."

## "I'm on Homes.com" (CoStar)

- **Membership** sorts the agent to the top of search with a larger card; member banner;
  enhanced bio on every listing. Placement is bought, not media-earned. [PAID]
  → homes.com/solutions/membership
- **Boost** bundles a **free Matterport 3D tour + interactive floor plan** (select markets)
  with premium placement and retargeting — i.e., the portal itself packages interactive media
  as "premium presentation." [PAID + OFFICIAL]  → homes.com/solutions/boost/agents
- **"Your listing, your lead":** only the listing agent's brand appears on the listing —
  so the media quality feeds *their* brand everywhere it's retargeted. [OFFICIAL]

## "I'm a Redfin agent" / Redfin-heavy market

- **Hot Home** badge is algorithmic and can't be bought: driven by *"tours scheduled,
  favorites for an individual home, and nearby market activity,"* re-evaluated daily. [OFFICIAL]
- Media's role is **[INFERENCE]**: great media plausibly drives the favorites/tour-requests
  the algorithm reads — Redfin never says photos trigger Hot Home. Say it honestly.
- Redfin includes pro photography + 3D walkthrough as **baseline** on its own listings —
  useful competitive line: "the discount brokerage treats pro media as table stakes." [OFFICIAL]

## "My listings live on Instagram" (the reel pitch)

From Meta's canonical ranking explainer (live official doc):
- Reels is the surface where *"the majority of what you see is from accounts you don't
  follow"* — the discovery pipeline; ranked on **likelihood to reshare and watch through**. [OFFICIAL]
- Instagram **officially suppresses** low-resolution, watermarked, bordered, or recycled
  video. [OFFICIAL] → A natively produced, high-res, unwatermarked 20-second vertical reel
  is rewarded *by policy*, not just by taste.
- Meta has never flatly said "Reels outrank photos" — don't claim it. The Socialinsider
  engagement gap (~40%, S7 in the main research file) is the honest companion stat.

## Google / YouTube

- Video earns search visibility **only when it's the main content of a page** (official,
  2023 policy). A reel buried mid-page earns nothing; a dedicated YouTube upload with
  VideoObject markup is eligible for video surfaces. [OFFICIAL]
- **Negative finding:** no Google rich-result support exists for virtual-tour/real-estate
  schema. Anyone selling "3D tour SEO boost" is making it up.

## What this changes for Sharp Sighted

1. The **Zillow 3D add-on** ($200–$800 tier) now has an official-platform-boost pitch behind
   it, and it requires a compatible 360 camera (Ricoh Theta / Insta360) + the Zillow 3D Home

   the Zillow *interactive* floor plan is the placement play. They're different deliverables —
   sell them as a ladder, not substitutes.

   Worth deciding whether to register (free Aryeo Lite) so "I'm Showcase-capable" is true
   the day an agent asks.
